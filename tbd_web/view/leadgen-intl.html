Why Choose Dubai?

  • Iconic Landmarks: Stand in awe of the Burj Khalifa, the tallest building in the world, or marvel at the man-made Palm Jumeirah.
  • Luxury Shopping: Explore the extravagant Dubai Mall and traditional Gold Souk for a shopping spree like no other.
  • Adventure Awaits: Enjoy adrenaline-pumping activities such as dune bashing, skydiving, and desert safaris.

5-Day Basic Itinerary for Dubai

Day 1: Arrival and Exploring Downtown Dubai

Morning: Arrive at Dubai International Airport and check into your hotel. Relax and refresh.

Afternoon: Head to the Dubai Mall for shopping and dining. Don’t miss the Dubai Aquarium & Underwater Zoo.

Evening: Visit the Burj Khalifa for a sunset view and enjoy the Dubai Fountain show.

Day 2: Desert Safari and Arabian Night

Morning: Explore the Al Fahidi Historical District and Dubai Museum to dive into the city’s heritage.

Afternoon: Embark on a desert safari with activities like dune bashing, camel riding, and sandboarding.

Evening: Enjoy a traditional Arabian dinner with live entertainment at a desert camp.

Day 3: Modern Marvels and Marina Cruise

Morning: Visit Palm Jumeirah and Atlantis The Palm. Explore The Lost Chambers Aquarium.

Afternoon: Stroll along Jumeirah Beach and take in views of the iconic Burj Al Arab.

Evening: Relax with a dhow cruise dinner along Dubai Marina.

Day 4: Family Fun and Cultural Immersion

Morning: Spend time at IMG Worlds of Adventure or Dubai Parks and Resorts for some family fun.

Afternoon: Visit the Spice and Gold Souks for unique souvenirs.

Evening: Savor local delicacies at a traditional Emirati restaurant.

Day 5: Departure

Morning: Relax at your hotel or indulge in last-minute shopping.

Afternoon: Head to the airport for your departure, carrying unforgettable memories of Dubai.

Highlights of Dubai

  • Burj Khalifa: The observation deck on the 148th floor offers breathtaking panoramic views of the city.
  • Desert Safari: Experience the mystique of the Arabian Desert with camel rides, sandboarding, and a traditional Bedouin-style dinner.
  • Dubai Marina: A bustling waterfront with stunning skyscrapers, luxury yachts, and a vibrant nightlife scene.
  • The Dubai Fountain: Witness mesmerizing water and light shows set to music at the base of the Burj Khalifa.
  • Old Dubai: Wander through the Al Fahidi Historical District, sip Arabic coffee, and take an abra ride across Dubai Creek.
  • Dubai Miracle Garden: A stunning floral paradise with over 45 million flowers arranged in vibrant patterns and displays.
  • Palm Jumeirah: A man-made island in the shape of a palm tree, home to luxurious resorts, including Atlantis The Palm, and a beautiful beach.
  • Dubai Aquarium & Underwater Zoo: One of the largest suspended aquariums, offering a chance to walk through an underwater tunnel surrounded by marine life.
  • Ski Dubai: An indoor ski resort in the Mall of the Emirates where you can enjoy skiing, snowboarding, and interacting with penguins.

Essential Travel Tips for Dubai

  • Best Time to Visit: November to March offers pleasant weather for outdoor activities.
  • Dress Modestly: While Dubai is modern, it’s respectful to dress conservatively in public areas.
  • Local Currency: The UAE Dirham (AED) is the currency. Credit cards are widely accepted.
  • Transportation: The Dubai Metro is efficient for city travel, and taxis are readily available.
  • Respect Local Customs: Be mindful of Emirati traditions, especially during Ramadan.
