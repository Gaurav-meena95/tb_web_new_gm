Find A Travel Buddy

Our proprietary algorithm matches potential travelers, local travel influencers, and travel providers based on their interests, location, behavioral, and demographic data. These personalized results are then shown on your social feed to enable you to make better travel decisions.

List Your Experience

Travel Buddy Marketplace allows travel providers and travel influencers to list their experiences on our platform and get them matched with travelers' demand. No hassle of page maintenance, SEO, EMI solutions, booking automation - it's all taken care of by Travel Buddy.

Want to make your good trip an unforgettable one? Travel Buddy Concierge offers bespoke local expert services offered via phone, chat, in person to travelers in any new destination. Don't just travel... Travel better - thanks to our local network!
