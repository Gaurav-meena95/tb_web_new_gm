Fare Estimator – Flight Price Prediction Tool

Fare Estimator by Travel Buddy is a free flight price forecasting tool that helps travelers find the cheapest days to fly. It analyzes airfare data across multiple months and presents it through intuitive visualizations.

  • Compare flight prices across 3, 6 and 9 months with interactive price trend graphs
  • Interactive calendar heatmap showing cheapest days to fly, color-coded by fare level
  • Automatic best-deal detection and ranking of the top 3 cheapest departure dates
  • Smart fare insights powered by historical airline pricing data
  • Supports Economy, Premium Economy, Business and First Class cabin comparison

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Fare Estimator?

Fare Estimator is a free flight price forecasting tool by Travel Buddy that helps travelers find the cheapest days to fly by analyzing airfare across 3, 6 and 9 months using interactive price trend graphs, calendar heatmaps and automatic best-deal detection.

How does Fare Estimator work?

It uses live airline pricing data to generate interactive price trend graphs, color-coded calendar heatmaps showing cheap vs expensive days, and automatically ranked best-deal recommendations for any route.

Can Fare Estimator predict flight prices?

Yes. Fare Estimator provides future fare estimates based on available airline data and historical pricing trends, helping you identify the cheapest travel dates up to 9 months in advance.

Is Fare Estimator free to use?

Yes, Fare Estimator is completely free. Simply enter your origin, destination and cabin class to instantly compare fares across multiple months.

What cabin classes does Fare Estimator support?

Fare Estimator supports all major cabin classes: Economy, Premium Economy, Business and First Class. You can compare fares for any class on any route.
