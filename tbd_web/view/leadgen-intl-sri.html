Why Visit Sri Lanka?

  • Rich Cultural Heritage:Sri Lanka boasts eight UNESCO World Heritage Sites, including the ancient cities of Anuradhapura and Polonnaruwa, the sacred city of Kandy, and the rock fortress of Sigiriya. These historical marvels provide a glimpse into the island's glorious past.
  • Breathtaking Landscapes:From the golden sands of Bentota to the rolling hills of Nuwara Eliya, Sri Lanka’s diverse terrain is a paradise for nature lovers. Don’t miss the chance to explore Horton Plains, Ella’s Nine Arches Bridge, and the verdant tea estates.
  • Wildlife Encounters:Sri Lanka is home to a stunning array of wildlife. Experience a thrilling safari at Yala National Park, known for its leopards, or visit Udawalawe to see herds of majestic elephants. Whale watching in Mirissa is another unforgettable experience.
  • Exotic Cuisine:Sri Lankan food is a burst of flavors. Relish local dishes like hoppers, kottu roti, and fragrant curries. The island’s tropical fruits and fresh seafood are a culinary delight.
  • Adventure and Relaxation:Whether you’re hiking to Adam’s Peak, surfing in Arugam Bay, or unwinding at a luxurious spa, Sri Lanka offers both adrenaline-pumping activities and serene escapes.

Highlights of Srilanka: Must-Visit Destinations

1. Sigiriya Rock Fortress

Climb the iconic Lion Rock to explore ancient frescoes, landscaped gardens, and breathtaking panoramic views.

2. Ella

A picturesque hill town known for the Nine Arches Bridge, tea plantations, and scenic hikes to Little Adam's Peak.

3. Yala National Park

Witness incredible wildlife, including leopards, elephants, and exotic birds, on an unforgettable safari experience.

4. Galle Fort

Stroll through this UNESCO World Heritage Site, blending colonial architecture with quaint cafes and boutique shops.

5. Temple of the Sacred Tooth Relic

Visit this revered Buddhist temple in Kandy, housing a relic of Lord Buddha's tooth.

6. Bentota Beach

Unwind on golden sands, enjoy water sports, and explore the serene Bentota River with mangrove tours.

7. Nuwara Eliya

Known as "Little England," this charming town offers lush tea estates, cool weather, and the picturesque Gregory Lake.

8. Mirissa

A paradise for whale watching, relaxing on the beach, and experiencing Sri Lanka's vibrant coastal life

9. Polonnaruwa

Step back in time to explore ancient ruins, temples, and stupas from Sri Lanka's rich historical heritage.

7-Day Itinerary for Srilanka

Day 1: Arrival in Colombo

Morning: Highlights: Bandaranaike International Airport, Galle Face Green, and Colombo Fort.

Activities: Arrive in Colombo, the vibrant capital city. Take a city tour to explore colonial-era architecture, bustling markets, and tranquil temples. Visit the National Museum and enjoy a sunset stroll along Galle Face Green.

Accommodation: Overnight stay in Colombo.

Day 2: Colombo to Sigiriya

Highlights: Travel to Sigiriya, a UNESCO World Heritage Site. Climb the iconic rock fortress to admire ancient frescoes and panoramic views. En route, visit the Dambulla Cave Temple, famous for its Buddhist statues and vibrant murals.

Activities: Overnight stay in Sigiriya.

Accommodation: Shop at MBK Center or Siam Paragon. Experience the vibrant nightlife at Khao San Road.

Day 3: Sigiriya to Kandy

Highlights: Temple of the Tooth Relic and Kandy Lake.

Activities: Journey to Kandy, the cultural capital of Sri Lanka. Explore the Temple of the Tooth Relic, a revered Buddhist site. Stroll around Kandy Lake and enjoy a traditional Kandyan dance performance in the evening.

Accommodation: Overnight stay in Kandy.

Day 4: Kandy to Nuwara Eliya

Highlights: Tea Plantations and Gregory Lake.

Activities: Drive to Nuwara Eliya, known as "Little England" for its colonial charm. Visit a tea factory to learn about tea production and sample some of the world’s finest teas. Spend a peaceful evening at Gregory Lake.

Accommodation: Overnight stay in Nuwara Eliya.

Day 5: Nuwara Eliya to Ella

Highlights: Train Journey and Nine Arches Bridge.

Activities: Board a scenic train to Ella, offering breathtaking views of lush greenery and waterfalls. Explore the picturesque Nine Arches Bridge and hike to Little Adam’s Peak for a spectacular sunset.

Accommodation: Overnight stay in Ella.

Day 6: Ella to Yala National Park

Highlights: Yala Safari.

Activities: Travel to Yala National Park for an exhilarating wildlife safari. Spot leopards, elephants, crocodiles, and a variety of bird species in their natural habitat.

Accommodation: Overnight stay near Yala National Park.

Day 7: Yala to Galle & Departure

Highlights: Galle Fort and Mirissa.

Activities: Visit the historic Galle Fort, a UNESCO World Heritage Site. Explore its cobblestone streets, boutique shops, and colonial buildings. If time permits, head to Mirissa for a relaxing beach experience or whale watching.

Accommodation: Drive back to Colombo for your departure flight.
