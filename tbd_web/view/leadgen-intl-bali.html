About Bali

Discover the Island of the Gods

Nestled in the heart of Indonesia, Bali is a tropical haven that captivates travelers with its breathtaking landscapes, vibrant culture, and serene beaches. Whether you're seeking spiritual rejuvenation, adrenaline-pumping activities, or a romantic escape, Bali promises an unforgettable experience.

From the golden sands of Kuta Beach to the lush rice terraces of Tegalalang, from ancient temples to modern beach clubs, Bali offers a perfect blend of tradition and contemporary luxury that appeals to all types of travelers.

Bali Attractions

Explore the best that Bali has to offer

Tanah Lot Temple

Perched on a rock formation, this iconic temple offers stunning sunset views and is one of Bali's most photographed landmarks.

Tegalalang Rice Terraces

Marvel at these lush, green terraces and take a famous swing ride for breathtaking views of the surrounding landscape.

Uluwatu Temple

Famous for its clifftop location and traditional Kecak dance performances against the backdrop of stunning ocean views.

Nusa Penida

Explore Kelingking Beach, Crystal Bay, and Angel's Billabong on this stunning island just off Bali's southeast coast.

Ubud Art Market

Shop for local crafts, textiles, and souvenirs in this vibrant market in the heart of Bali's cultural center.

Sekumpul Waterfall

Considered Bali's most beautiful waterfall, perfect for nature lovers and adventure seekers.

Your 7-Day Bali Itinerary

A carefully crafted journey to experience the best of Bali

Day 1: Arrival in Bali

Upon arrival at Bali International Airport Ngurah Rai (DPS), you'll be greeted by our representative who will assist with your transfer to the hotel. Enjoy the rest of the evening at your leisure, relaxing and settling in after your journey.

Day 2: Ubud & Kintamani Tour

Embark on a Kintamani tour showcasing the stunning northeast Bali, highlighted by breathtaking views of Mount Batur volcano and surrounding lakes. Visit traditional markets and the hidden beauty of Tukad Cepung Waterfall.

Day 3: Tanah Lot & Uluwatu

Witness the mesmerizing Kecak dance at Uluwatu Temple, set against the temple's cliff-edge backdrop. Later, visit the iconic Tanah Lot Temple, perched on a rock in the ocean, offering picturesque sunset views.

Day 4: North Bali Exploration

Head to Bedugul Mountain resort, known for its stunning vistas of Lake Beratan and the iconic Ulun Danu Temple. Explore Mengwi and continue to Jatiluwih to admire its breathtaking UNESCO-recognized rice terraces.

Day 5: Leisure Day

Today, you have the freedom to discover Bali at your own pace, whether exploring local towns, immersing yourself in its vibrant culture, or delving into its rich heritage. Optional activities can be arranged upon request.

Day 6: Leisure Day

Another day at your leisure to enjoy Bali's beaches, spas, shopping, or any activities you may have missed. Our team can recommend personalized experiences based on your interests.

Day 7: Departure

Check out from your hotel and prepare for departure. Our team will transfer you to Bali International Airport Ngurah Rai (DPS) for your return flight home. Safe travels!
